About COCO

Build AI Teams.
Deploy in Minutes.

AI Agent Infrastructure for the Human × Agent era

COCO is dedicated to turning the Human × Agent (HxA) vision into enterprise-grade productivity. We believe Agents are not just tools — they are partners that collaborate with humans as equals. Through the HxA Enterprise Service System, COCO helps businesses build Agent Teams composed of both humans and AI Agents — where Agents have their own identities, roles, and collaborative relationships, just like employees, driving continuous business operations.
